Maintain member details

This allows you to publish minutes. They may optionally be reviewed by the Wags committee before publication

  1. In order to display the minutes on the web site, they must first be saved in "pdf" format.

    In Word, once the minutes are ready, click on File, then Save As, then choose PDF from the drop-down list.

  2. Go to www.wags.org/wagsadmin
  3. Click on "Minutes"
  4. Enter the meeting date. This will form the name of the file once it is uploaded to the Wag site.
  5. Optionally, enter a message.
  6. Click on Choose File and navigate to the saved PDF file.
     

    either

  7. Click on Submit. This will upload the file to a drafts area and send an email to all committee members with the message and a link.

    or

  8. Click on Publish. This will upload the file to the minutes area and create a News item with the message that you give.
