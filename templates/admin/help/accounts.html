Accounts

This allows you to update all members accounts to reflect the current contents of the accounts Excel workbook. It also allows you to update the Hole in One Fund figures shown on the web site front page.

Update member accounts

  1. Load the Excel accounts workbook
  2. Depending on your Excel version

    either:

    • Click on the "Developer" tab
    • Click on "Macros" (on the left)

    or:

    • Click on "View"
    • Click on "Macros" (down arrow) and View Macros
  3. Select "ExtractAllTrans" and click "Run"

    This creates a file called "accounts.tab" in the same directory as the workbook

  4. In your browser, go to wags.org/wagsadmin. If you are updating the account for a previous year go to wags.org/wagsadmin/{year} where {year} is the year you wish to update e.g. 2020
  5. Click on "Accounts" at the top
  6. Click on Choose file and navigate to the workbook directory and select the "accounts.tab" file.
  7. Click on Upload This should respond with "file uploaded successfully"
  8. Go to News and add a new Accounts Updated item

Update hole in one funds

  1. In your browser, go to wags.org/wagsadmin
  2. Click on "Accounts" at the top
  3. Enter new values for the Hole in one fund
  4. Click on Update

    This should respond with "Hole in one fund updated successfully"
